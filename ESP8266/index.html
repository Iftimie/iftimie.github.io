Desk Companion - ESP8266 Experiments

A small desk companion concept: a simple OLED screen with two touch inputs that greets you, shares random facts, and slowly reveals a mysterious story through droid-like audio clips. The idea is a branching narrative where your button choices change how the companion responds.

Concept

It starts with a friendly boot-up and random facts, but a strange noise appears. The companion discovers memory corruption and hidden messages. With only two buttons, the story branches into different interpretations of the entity communicating through the device.
