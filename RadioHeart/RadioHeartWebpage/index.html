Phones demand attention.

This doesn’t.

The story

Presence, revealed slowly.

It’s emotionally under-specified tech.

Presence without demand.

Phones are aggressive.

buzz

ping

interrupt

escalate anxiety

This does the opposite.

no text

no sound

no obligation

no “answer now”

Just:

“Something that matters to you exists nearby.”

Ambient reassurance.

We wanted a way to feel close —

without checking a screen.

So we built a small object

that doesn’t ask for your attention.

When the other half is near,

it breathes light.

When it’s not,

it rests.

Not communication.

Not productivity.

Not safety.

No messages.

No noise.

No pressure.

Just a quiet reminder

that someone you love exists —

right now.

For couples who live together

but drift between rooms.

For couples who are far,

or simply tired of phones.

Disconnected from the internet.

Limited range (~1 km).

You are not selling utility.

You are selling permission to feel

without interruption.

A quiet way to feel close, without talking.

The ritual

Slow, simple, shared.

  1. Open together.
  2. Each of you take one heart.
  3. Connect it to power.
  4. Press the button at the same time.

When it glows, the connection is made.

What you get

Comes as a pair.
USB charged.
Designed to be quiet.
No app required.
